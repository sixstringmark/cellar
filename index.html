Psychedelic Cellar
Home Links About
This is the home of the Vinyl Vortex Psychedelic Cellar internet radio show. Streaming is absolutely free with no registration required. Shows are divided into parts roughly one hour long. To stream a show part, press the button for the show then press the "Play" button in the part heading in the contents.
This site features the music of the creative explosion of the psychedelic era of the later sixties and early seventies. You'll hear a a variety of music - rock, folk, jazz, classical, and roots music, along some music that is beyond category. The mix includes some of the best known music of the era, along with some rare and obscure songs. The presentation is in the style of the "underground" FM radio stations of the time, with spoken information about the artist, title, album, year, and some background information about the music being played.
